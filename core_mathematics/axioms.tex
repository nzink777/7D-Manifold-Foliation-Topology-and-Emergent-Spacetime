This module establishes the five foundational geometric axioms governing the 7D-to-4D manifold foliation architecture. These axioms replace static background continuum assumptions with a purely topological, intersection-driven framework.

% =========================================================================
\begin{axiom}[The Bulk Manifold]
% [NODE: AXIOM_BULK_MANIFOLD]
The background cosmic information field is a smooth, compact, non-singular 7-dimensional torus defined as the product space of seven circles:
\begin{equation}
    \mathcal{M}^7 \cong T^7 = \prod_{A=1}^{7} S^1_A = (S^1)^7
\end{equation}
The global topology possesses an Euler characteristic $\chi(T^7) = 0$ and Betti numbers $\beta_n = \binom{7}{n}$, ensuring a completely stable, vector-field-symmetric background field that acts as a global informational condenser.
\begin{equation}
    \det(g_{AB}) \neq 0 \quad \forall x \in T^7
\end{equation}
\end{axiom}

% =========================================================================
\begin{axiom}[Structural Objects]
% [NODE: AXIOM_STRUCTURAL_OBJECTS]
Persistent physical invariants within the bulk space are represented by smooth embeddings of closed 1-manifolds (Lissajous knots) $\gamma: S^1 \hookrightarrow T^7$. To maintain topological closure and structural preservation under ambient isotopy, the intrinsic winding frequencies $\omega_A \in \mathbb{Z}$ across all seven toroidal dimensions must satisfy the global harmonic congruence:
\begin{equation}
    \sum_{A=1}^{7} \omega_A \equiv 0 \pmod 7
\end{equation}
\end{axiom}

% =========================================================================
\begin{axiom}[Emergent Spacetime Slicing]
% [NODE: AXIOM_SPACETIME_SLICING]
Macroscopic spacetime does not exist as an independent absolute construct. It manifests strictly as a parameterized, smoothly moving family of embedded 4-dimensional submanifolds $\mathcal{M}^4(\tau) \subset T^7$, where the monotonic real parameter $\tau \in \mathbb{R}$ governs the evolution of the foliation layer and maps directly to macroscopic cosmological time.
\end{axiom}

% =========================================================================
\begin{axiom}[The Particle Intersection Operator]
% [NODE: AXIOM_INTERSECTION_OPERATOR]
Observable elementary matter packets are not localized points or independent vibrating strings embedded *into* spacetime. They are defined strictly as the discrete, transverse topological intersections of the 7D structural knot $\gamma(S^1)$ with the translating macroscopic 4D slicing plane $\mathcal{M}^4(\tau)$:
\begin{equation}
    \mathcal{P}(\tau) = \gamma(S^1) \cap \mathcal{M}^4(\tau) = \{ t_k(\tau) \in S^1 \mid \gamma(t_k) \in \mathcal{M}^4(\tau) \}
\end{equation}
The continuous translation of these transcendental root coordinates $t_k(\tau)$ maps out the smooth, deterministic worldlines of observable particles.
\end{axiom}

\begin{axiom}[Gauge Dynamics]

\end{axiom}
